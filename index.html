Usage & Licensing

You own the
rights you need.

Commercial photography involves two separate fees: the creative fee (the shoot itself) and the usage license (how and where the images are used). This is standard industry practice and protects both parties.

Licensing is based on usage scope — social media content costs differently than a national ad campaign. I keep it transparent and flexible. We agree on everything upfront, in writing.

Digital / Social Owned channels, social media, website, email marketing. Time-limited or perpetual.
Advertising Paid media — digital ads, OOH, print campaigns. Scoped by territory and duration.
Editorial Press, editorial features, PR use. Non-commercial distribution.
Exclusive Buyout Full rights transfer. Available for select projects — discussed case by case.
